Alternative Simulation Engines

  • 🎮
    Unreal Engine 5 (Water System)

    Highest visual fidelity. Can import 3D meshes and run complex wave kinematics and fluid dynamics. Excellent for stakeholder presentations, though highly computationally expensive.

  • 🌊
    Unity + Crest Ocean System

    Strong alternative to UE5. Crest provides physically based shallow water equations suitable for coastline wave breaking and mangrove collision interactions.

  • 📊
    HEC-RAS (2D) + Blender

    Industry standard for hydrological accuracy. Run the numerical math in HEC-RAS, export the water surface elevation arrays, and animate the realistic rendering in Blender.

Open Data Sources for the Bahamas

To populate your digital twin, utilize these repositories for baseline topography and bathymetry.

  • OpenTopography

    Excellent for global DEMs (Copernicus 30m, NASADEM). Can often find higher resolution regional subsets uploaded by academic researchers.

  • GEBCO (General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans)

    Essential for the underwater terrain required to accurately model wave shoaling before it hits the mangroves.

  • NOAA Digital Coast

    While US-focused, often contains datasets extending into the Caribbean and Bahamas, including sea level rise viewers and raw LIDAR point clouds.

  • UNEP-WCMC (Ocean+ Habitats)

    Crucial for obtaining spatial polygon data of existing mangrove distributions in the Bahamas to base your "Before/After" models on.
